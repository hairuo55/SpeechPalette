SpeechPalette: A Comprehensive High-quality Speech Editing Method for Text-based Speech Editing, One-shot TTS, and Attributes Editing

Tao Wang (tao.wang@nlpr.ia.ac.cn)

School of Artificial Intelligence, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China

Messages

In this paper, we present SpeechPalette, a comprehensive speech editing method that allows users to easily modify various attributes of the selected speech segment according to their preferences, which includes text-based speech editing, one-shot TTS, pitch and speed rate adjustments, etc.

This page shows the samples in the paper "SpeechPalette: A Comprehensive High-quality Speech Editing Method for Text-based Speech Editing, One-shot TTS, and Attributes Editing".

Experiments were based on LibriTTS. All test samples and speakers have not appeared in the training set and validation set.
